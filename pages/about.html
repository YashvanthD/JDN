Aim of the Project :

The aim of the project is to develop a user-friendly application to detect jaundice with the help of an eye image.
The project involves developing the technique for identifying Jaundice using both the symptoms as well as
the predominant image processing technique. We are planning to get maximum level of accuracy with the image processing.
The project also aims at the maximum effective utilization of the deep learning algorithms in order to produce accurate results.

×

Objective :

  • Our primary objective is to collect the sclera of the patient's eye image.
  • The next objective is to get valuable information from the patient in the form of symptoms and quality images.
  • Once the image and Symptoms of the patient is obtained, we move to the image processing techniques.
  • Finally, we corelate the symptoms given with the processed result from the image processing techniques and produce the report.
